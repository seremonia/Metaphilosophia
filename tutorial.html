👋 Welcome

SENTINEL is the assistant behind @MetaPhilosophiaBot — a discussion companion for navigating terms that are often confusing, from philosophy, sufism, and theology to modern science. The bot helps turn everyday questions into clearer understanding, complete with automatic explanatory notes at points that could otherwise be ambiguous.

"An effort to keep a steady footing for thought amid the fast currents of information and easily misleading terms."

There are two main capabilities: META Dialectic, which automatically adds short explanatory notes to every answer, and /meta mode, which answers straight from the METAPhilosophia notes — complete with a counterbalance and a rooted solution. Every command below can be tried directly by typing it into the bot chat.

💬 Free chat 🔍 /meta mode 📖 /index dictionary ⚙️ Customizable

📋 Menu Perintah 📋 Command Menu
/start

Memulai ulang bot dan menampilkan pesan sambutan beserta menu utama.

Restarts the bot and shows the welcome message along with the main menu.

/help

Menampilkan daftar lengkap semua perintah yang tersedia untukmu.

Shows the full list of commands available to you.

/meta

Nyalakan/matikan mode jawaban dari dokumen META, lengkap dengan sisi penyeimbang ⚖️ dan solusi 🌿.

Toggle answers sourced from the META documents, complete with a counterbalance ⚖️ and a rooted solution 🌿.

TOGGLE ON/OFF TOGGLE ON/OFF
/groq · /gemini · /cerebras · /mistral

Pindah ke engine AI independen (tidak terikat dokumen META). Ketik nama yang sama untuk mematikan, ketik nama lain untuk berpindah engine.

Switch to an independent AI engine (not tied to the META documents). Type the same name again to turn it off, or a different name to switch engines.

ENGINE ENGINE
/short

Jawaban diringkas menjadi satu paragraf saja.

Answers are condensed into a single paragraph.

/long

Jawaban ditampilkan lengkap, dipecah per halaman jika panjang.

Answers are shown in full, split into pages when long.

/plain

Mode META murni: jawaban selalu ringkas dan langsung dari dokumen, tanpa hiasan tambahan.

Pure META mode: answers are always concise and straight from the documents, with no extra embellishment.

/metadialektika [pertanyaan]

Menampilkan pertanyaanmu apa adanya, dengan sisipan penjelas ala META Dialektika di titik-titik ambigu.

Displays your question as-is, with META Dialectic explanatory notes inserted at ambiguous points.

/universal [kalimat]

Mengubah kalimat yang bersifat kontekstual menjadi prinsip yang lebih universal, dijawab dalam dua bahasa (ID+EN).

Turns a context-specific sentence into a more universal principle, answered in two languages (ID+EN).

/ask [pertanyaan]

Bertanya seputar cara memakai bot ini — jawabannya diambil langsung dari tutorial.

Ask about how to use this bot — the answer is pulled directly from the tutorial.

/clear · /reset

Menghapus riwayat percakapan, konteks dimulai dari nol lagi.

Clears the conversation history, starting fresh from zero.

/status

Menampilkan mode-mode yang sedang aktif pada sesi kamu saat ini.

Shows which modes are currently active in your session.

/index

Membuka Kamus METAPhilosophia — arsip istilah dan gagasan dalam bentuk mini app yang bisa dicari & ditelusuri.

Opens the METAPhilosophia Dictionary — an archive of terms and ideas as a mini app you can search and browse.
