## 🇮🇩 Bahasa Indonesia

### KEBIJAKAN METAFILSAFAT

Mengikuti diskusi ini untuk melihat istilah baru (dikenal di) filsafat / tasawuf / kalam dan meluruskan pemahamannya seharusnya bagaimana.

Ini perlu agar pihak yang baru saja memahami istilah asing tak terjebak mengira sebagai benar. Melainkan harus dikembalikan ke pemahaman yang lurus sesuai Al-Qur'an & Hadits dan ungkapan mahsyur dari ulama yang memahami Al-Qur'an & Hadits.

Jadi penggunaan istilah intelektual / filsafat / kalam, bukan berarti saya sepaham dengannya, melainkan mendudukkan posisinya harus dipahami seperti bagaimana.

### ISTILAH MISTIK & INTELEKTUAL

Ini perlu disikapi karena banyak muncul istilah baru atau istilah lama yang tak dikenal di Islam seperti "Tajali", "Emanasi", "Cermin", "Ittihad", "Hulul" atau istilah filsafat seperti "Potensi", "Aktualisasi", "Sebab Relatif", "Sebab Mutlak" dan lainnya — yang beberapa merupakan akibat dari pengaruh (penyalahgunaan) filsafat, atau pihak anti-agama yang mendebat dengan istilah logika "relatif" & "sebab akibat".

### ISTILAH SAINS

Belum lagi istilah sains seperti superposisi, quantum entanglement, dan lainnya, yang jika gagal dipahami karena bias kognitif, bisa tumpang tindih (atau sengaja ditumpang-tindihkan oleh pihak tertentu) dengan keimanan agama, sehingga beresiko mengaburkan keimanan.

Seperti contoh "energi tak diciptakan kecuali hanya berubah". Konsep sains seperti ini bisa disalahpahami sebagai "Tuhan itu energi", padahal tidak demikian. Belum lagi konsep dari mistik seperti "Wahdatul Wujud", "Cermin", "Tajali" — yang jika tak dijelaskan bagaimana seharusnya dipahami, justru bisa disalahpahami. Belum lagi istilah lain seperti "Flow State", "Affordance", dan lainnya.

### MELIHAT DIBALIK MAKNA / KONSEP

Di sini dicoba diluruskan sehingga tak terjebak makna/konsep di balik istilah. Mau pakai istilah apa pun dalam diskusi, kita tetap memahami makna/konsep di baliknya — sehingga dalam diskusi atau saat mengambil pelajaran, tak terjebak menerima makna di balik istilah, dan istilah baru yang (tak) populer atau dianggap kontroversial menjadi tak penting lagi. Bahkan dalam diskusi tak terjebak perdebatan definisi suatu istilah, melainkan kita bisa mengganti dengan istilah lain tanpa keluar konteks. Mengapa ini penting?

### MENEMUKAN PEGANGAN

Karena kita bisa memahami struktur di balik kata/bahasa, membuka ambiguitas, dan setelah diketahui struktur makna hakikinya, memudahkan merujuk ke pemahaman yang sesuai keimanan tanpa terjebak tumpang tindih permainan istilah.

❇️ Ini melibatkan kebenaran mutlak universal, agar ketika menghadapi triliunan konsep/makna/istilah/simbolik, tak perlu diatasi semuanya yang relatif banyak — melainkan semua secara otomatis tersaring dengan filter kebenaran universal, sehingga dari jutaan ambiguitas ternyata dapat diketahui hanya beberapa saja, tak sebanyak dugaan sebelumnya.

✅ Ini penting agar tak mudah dikisruhkan atau tak mudah dibuat waswas karena ambiguitas yang diolah kesannya bertumpuk-tumpuk, padahal kasus yang sama diulang-ulang dengan narasi berbeda.

✅ Ini akan memberi kelegaan, tak terjebak dalam keterasingan istilah — bahkan melihatnya sebagai batas: apakah sesuatu itu diterima sebagai hal baru yang inspiratif, atau memang suatu keterasingan yang perlu diwaspadai.

💠 Bahasa sederhananya: agar kita selalu punya pegangan keimanan di antara keterasingan di arus deras informasi global yang bisa menjebak.

## 🇬🇧 English

### METAFILSAFAT POLICY

This discussion is followed in order to identify new terminology (as known in) philosophy / Sufism / Islamic theology (kalam), and to correct/clarify how it should properly be understood.

This is necessary so that anyone newly encountering an unfamiliar term does not get trapped into assuming it is automatically correct. Instead, it must be brought back to a proper understanding in line with the Qur'an & Hadith, and the well-known statements of scholars who understand the Qur'an & Hadith.

So the use of intellectual / philosophical / theological terminology does not mean I agree with it — rather, it is meant to establish how its position should properly be understood.

### MYSTICAL & INTELLECTUAL TERMS

This needs to be addressed because many new terms, or old terms unfamiliar in Islam, keep appearing — such as "Tajalli," "Emanation," "Mirror," "Ittihad," "Hulul," or philosophical terms like "Potentiality," "Actualization," "Relative Cause," "Absolute Cause," and others. Some of these arise from the influence (or misuse) of philosophy, or from anti-religion parties who argue using the logic of "relativity" and "cause and effect."

### SCIENTIFIC TERMS

Then there are scientific terms such as superposition, quantum entanglement, and others, which — if misunderstood due to cognitive bias — can be conflated (or deliberately conflated by certain parties) with religious faith, risking the obscuring of true belief.

For example: "energy is not created, it only changes form." A scientific concept like this can be misinterpreted as "God is energy" — which is not the case. There are also mystical concepts such as "Wahdat al-Wujud" (Unity of Being), "Mirror," "Tajalli" — which, if not properly explained, can easily be misunderstood. Not to mention other terms like "Flow State," "Affordance," and so on.

### LOOKING BEYOND THE MEANING / CONCEPT

Here, an attempt is made to set things straight so we don't get trapped by the meaning/concept behind a term. Whatever terminology is used in a discussion, we still aim to understand the meaning/concept behind it — so that in discussion, or when drawing lessons, we don't get trapped accepting the meaning behind a term at face value, and a new term — whether popular or not, or considered controversial — becomes no longer important in itself. Discussions should not even get trapped in debating the definition of a term; instead, we can substitute it with another term without leaving the context. Why does this matter?

### FINDING A GROUNDING

Because we can understand the structure behind words/language, which opens up ambiguity — and once the true structural meaning is known, it becomes easier to refer back to an understanding consistent with faith, without getting trapped in the overlapping play of terminology.

❇️ This involves absolute universal truth, so that when facing trillions of concepts/meanings/terms/symbols, we don't need to address all of that relatively large number one by one — instead, everything is automatically filtered through the lens of universal truth, so that out of millions of ambiguities, it turns out only a few genuinely matter — far fewer than initially assumed.

✅ This matters so we are not easily thrown into confusion or made anxious by ambiguity that appears to pile up, when in fact the same case is simply repeated with a different narrative.

✅ This brings relief — not getting trapped in the alienation of terminology, and even seeing it as a boundary: is something accepted as genuinely new and inspiring, or is it actually a form of alienation that needs to be approached with caution?

💠 In simple terms: so that we always have a grounding of faith amid the alienation within the fast-flowing, potentially misleading current of global information.
